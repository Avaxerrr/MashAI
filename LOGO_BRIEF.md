# MashAI Logo Design Brief

> This document provides comprehensive context for AI image generation tools to create a logo for MashAI.

---

## Brand Overview

**Product Name:** MashAI  
**Tagline:** Your Unified AI Workspace  
**Category:** Desktop Application / Productivity Tool / AI Browser

### What MashAI Does
MashAI is a desktop application that combines multiple AI chatbot services (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, Grok) into a single, organized workspace. It's like having a dedicated browser just for AI tools, with features like:
- Multi-profile organization (Work vs Personal)
- Smart tab management with memory optimization
- Quick access to any AI provider from one interface

### Core Value Proposition
**"One workspace. All AI assistants. Total focus."**

---

## Design Direction

### Concept Keywords
- **Unified** – Bringing multiple things together as one
- **AI/Intelligence** – Artificial intelligence, smart, neural
- **Workspace** – Productive, organized, professional
- **Mash/Blend** – Combining, merging, fusion
- **Modern** – Clean, minimal, contemporary
- **Tech-forward** – Cutting edge, innovative

### Visual Metaphors to Consider
1. **Convergence** – Multiple streams merging into one
2. **Brain/Neural Network** – Representing AI intelligence
3. **Tabs/Windows** – Browser-like interface element
4. **Mosaic/Grid** – Multiple parts forming a whole
5. **Infinity/Loop** – Continuous, unified experience
6. **Spark/Lightning** – Quick access, instant intelligence

### Name Analysis: "MashAI"
- **"Mash"** = To combine, blend, merge (like a mashup)
- **"AI"** = Artificial Intelligence
- The name suggests a **blend/fusion of AI services**

## Style Guidelines

### Color Palette Suggestions

**Primary Options:**
| Color | Hex | Meaning |
|-------|-----|---------|
| Deep Purple | `#8B5CF6` | AI/Tech, creativity, intelligence |
| Electric Blue | `#3B82F6` | Trust, technology, clarity |
| Teal/Cyan | `#06B6D4` | Innovation, freshness |
| Gradient Purple to Blue | `#8B5CF6 to #3B82F6` | Modern tech aesthetic |

**Accent Options:**
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Vibrant Pink | `#EC4899` | Energy, AI spark |
| Lime | `#84CC16` | Growth, activity |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Clean iconography |

**Background Context:**
- App uses a **dark theme** (VS Code inspired: `#1e1e1e`, `#252526`)
- Logo should work on both dark and light backgrounds
- Consider transparency-ready versions

### Typography Style
- Modern sans-serif
- Clean, geometric letterforms
- Consider custom ligature for "AI" portion
- Bold but not heavy

### Icon Style
- **Geometric/Abstract** preferred
- Clean lines, minimal detail
- Works at small sizes (16px favicon up to full logo)
- Single-color version should be recognizable

---

## Logo Requirements

### Formats Needed
1. **App Icon** – Square, works at 16x16 to 512x512
2. **Horizontal Logo** – Icon + wordmark
3. **Icon Only** – For favicon, tray icon, taskbar
4. **Wordmark Only** – For contexts where icon isn't needed

### Technical Requirements
- Must be recognizable at 16x16 pixels (system tray)
- Should work on dark backgrounds (primary use case)
- Clean silhouette for single-color usage
- No gradients required for small sizes

---

## What to Avoid

- Generic chat bubble icons (too common)
- Literal robot faces (outdated AI trope)
- Too many colors (keep it simple)
- Overly complex details (won't scale down)
- Cliche AI imagery (brains with circuits)
- Copying existing AI company logos
